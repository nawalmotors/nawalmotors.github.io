About Us

Nawal Motors is a leading entrant in the automobile sector. It was established in the month of May, 1995. It is presently integrated with Tata Motors as an authorized dealer of passenger cars. It is located at No C11, Site A, Industrial Area, Mathura, Uttar Pradesh. The Dealership have well equipped Service Center with EV Charging Facility. Nawal Motors has range of TATA passenger car models across segments- TIAGO, TIGOR, ALTROZ, PUNCH, NEXON, SAFARI, HARRIER.
